Decentralised Rating System

The current reputation system is dependent on centralised players which makes it more prone to manipulation. Thus by virtue of karm.network we strive to build a decentralised and open reputation utility service, which is not limited to an industry or entity or service or product but rather which en-compasses all of the above.

Incentivizing Act of Rating

Our platform aligns the users with the brand/product by giving them skin-in-the-game via rewarding every Act-of-Rating (AOR). A user remains protected by anonymity by the protocol whatever rating output may be.

Reputation AS NFT

Our platform allows a brand/product/service/ person to get transformed into an online ownership entity by building an NFT (non-fungible token) that attracts ratings by the users. Over time, the value of this reputation entity grows and gets embedded inside the digital property.
